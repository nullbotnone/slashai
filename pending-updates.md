- AI features designed to improve Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) performance with tools like AEO Grader
- HubSpot offering AI at no extra cost and adopting seat pricing to encourage adoption

### 🚀 Semrush
- Launched "Spotlight 2026" — AI-focused marketing conference centered on brand visibility in AI search era

- Developments align with ongoing acquisition process with Adobe

- Emphasis on optimizing for inclusion in AI overviews, building trust signals, monitoring citation performance
- Website traffic from AI search may surpass traditional search traffic by 2028
- AI Visibility Toolkit and SEO Toolkit help users monitor AI mentions, visibility, share of voice, sentiment, engagement from AI-driven traffic

### 🚀 Surfer SEO
- Focusing on AI visibility and search optimization
- Releasing "Sites" — new SEO hub
- Updated Outlines tab in Content Editor
- Improved filters in Dashboards
- Enhanced WordPress plugin
- Key new feature: AI Tracker — data-backed AI visibility tool to monitor/control what AI models say about a brand

- Content Editor provides real-time "Content Score" based on topic coverage, keyword usage, alignment with top-ranking pages
- Added new AI Search guidelines to Content Editor (facts AI models need to trust and cite content)
- Can apply guidelines individually or use "Auto-Optimize" for one-click optimization for both SERP and AI answers
- Surfer AI allows quick generation of AI SEO-ready content (human editing/fact-checking still recommended)
- Other features: Content Audit tool for updating old content, Keyword Research tool

- Vast voice library: over 200 high-quality AI voices mimicking human speech patterns
- Emphasis on second-generation Text-to-Speech (TTS) models delivering hyper-realistic voice synthesis capturing subtle inflections and rhythms

- Offers Voice Changer feature to enhance raw audio
- Integrations with tools like Canva, Google Slides, PowerPoint, Articulate 360
- Highlights ethical sourcing of AI voices: models trained on real voice actors who consent and receive royalties
