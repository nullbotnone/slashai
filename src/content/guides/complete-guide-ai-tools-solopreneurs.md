## AI Writing Tools

Writing is the #1 time sink for solopreneurs. AI can handle 80% of it.

### For Budget-Conscious ($0–20/mo)
- **ChatGPT Free/Plus** — Best all-rounder. Handles emails, proposals, blog posts, and research. The $20/mo Plus plan is worth it if you write daily.
- **Claude Free/Pro** — Better for long documents and nuanced writing. Use the free tier for editing and analysis.
- **Rytr** ($9/mo) — Cheapest quality AI writer. 40+ use cases, great for freelancers on tight budgets.

### For Serious Content Creators ($20–50/mo)
- **Jasper AI** ($49/mo) — Best AI writing tool for daily writers. Brand Voice feature learns your style. Surfer SEO integration for ranking content.
- **Writesonic** ($19/mo) — Best value. ChatSonic with web access, bulk generation, 80+ templates.
- **Copy.ai** ($49/mo) — Best for marketing copy. 90+ templates with workflow automation.

### For Everyone
- **Grammarly** (Free–$12/mo) — Essential companion to any AI writer. Catches errors, improves tone, works everywhere.

## AI Design Tools

You don't need to hire a designer. AI handles most visual needs.

### Best Options
- **Canva** (Free–$13/mo) — The Swiss Army knife of design. Templates for everything. AI image generation, Magic Resize, Brand Kit.
- **Midjourney** ($10/mo) — Best image quality. Generate unique visuals for blogs, social media, and client work.
- **Gamma** (Free–$8/mo) — Generate full presentations from a text prompt. Perfect for client decks.
- **Framer** (Free–$10/mo) — Build websites from text descriptions. No coding needed.

### When to Use Which
- **Social media graphics** → Canva
- **Unique images/art** → Midjourney
- **Presentations** → Gamma
- **Website** → Framer

## AI Automation Tools

Automation is where solopreneurs get leverage. Do something once, let AI repeat it forever.

### Start Here (Free)
- **Zapier** (100 free tasks/mo) — Connect your apps. New email → add to spreadsheet → send welcome email.
- **Make.com** (1,000 free operations/mo) — More powerful than Zapier for complex workflows.
- **Google Workspace Studio** (Free with Google Workspace) — Automate Gmail, Sheets, and Drive.

### Scale Up ($19–49/mo)
- **Lindy AI** ($49/mo) — The most autonomous AI agent. Handles email triage, scheduling, CRM updates. Set-and-forget.
- **n8n** (Free self-hosted) — Open-source automation with AI agent nodes. Full control, no vendor lock-in.

## The Golden Rules of AI for Solopreneurs

1. **Start free, upgrade selectively.** Most tools have generous free tiers. Prove value before paying.

2. **Master 3-5 tools, not 20.** Deep expertise with a few tools beats shallow knowledge of many.

3. **AI drafts, you edit.** Never ship raw AI output. Your voice and quality control are your brand.

4. **Automate what you repeat.** If you do something more than twice, automate it.

5. **Track ROI.** If a tool doesn't save you at least 5x its cost in time or revenue, drop it.

6. **Stay human.** In a world of AI content, your personality, opinions, and relationships are your edge.
