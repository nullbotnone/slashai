## What Is Surfer SEO Sites?

Surfer SEO Sites is an all-in-one hub introduced in April 2026 that serves as a central dashboard for managing your entire SEO workflow. ## Setup and Usage

1. Connect your Google Search Console account to Surfer SEO
2. Select the properties (websites) you want to monitor in Sites
3. Allow Surfer to pull in data (may take a few hours for initial sync)
4. Navigate to the Sites section in Surfer SEO dashboard
5. Use the dashboard to:
   - View performance metrics for your content
   - Identify underperforming pages for optimization
   - Explore new content ideas via Topical Map
   - Access AI Visibility tools and recommendations
6. Click on any page to jump directly to the Content Editor for optimization

## Cons

- Not a replacement for dedicated rank tracking tools (limited historical data)

- Learning curve to navigate the new dashboard and features

- Best suited for content-focused SEO strategies rather than technical SEO

## Who Should Use Surfer SEO Sites

**Surfer SEO Sites is worth it if:**
- You create content regularly and want to measure its performance in Google Search
- You want to streamline your SEO workflow from audit to optimization
- You're interested in tracking AI visibility alongside traditional SEO
- You work with a team and need to manage access to client data
- You already use Surfer SEO and want to leverage its latest capabilities

**Surfer SEO Sites might be less critical if:**
- You focus primarily on technical SEO and site audits
- You rely on other tools for rank tracking and are satisfied with their workflow
- Your website has very low traffic (GSC data may be too sparse for meaningful insights)
- You don't create content and instead focus on link building or technical fixes
