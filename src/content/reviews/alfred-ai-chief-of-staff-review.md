## What Is Alfred_?

Alfred_ (get-alfred.ai) is an AI Chief of Staff or AI Executive Assistant designed to autonomously manage email, calendar, and tasks for founders, consultants, and executives. It operates 24/7 in the background, learning user preferences and communication patterns to provide a prioritized Daily Brief each morning.

Unlike AI chatbots that answer questions, Alfred_ acts as a virtual employee that plans, decides, and executes multi-step tasks while you sleep—triaging your inbox, drafting replies, extracting action items, scheduling meetings, and ensuring nothing falls through the cracks.

## How It Works

Using Alfred_ feels like having a dedicated executive assistant who never sleeps. The process breaks down into several key functions:

### 1. Email Command
Alfred_ connects securely to your Gmail or Outlook account via OAuth 2.0 and processes your inbox overnight. It:
- Triages emails by urgency and importance
- Drafts replies in your voice and tone
- Flags emails requiring human judgment
- Archives less critical communications
- Extracts tasks and action items automatically

### 2. Calendar Intelligence
The AI analyzes your schedule for conflicts, identifies and helps protect focus time, and connects meetings to related emails and tasks. It assesses the "health" of each day based on meeting load and suggests optimal times for deep work.

### 3. Task Extraction
Alfred_ automatically pulls action items from emails and messages, creates tasks with deadlines and context, and tracks them in its internal task manager. It offers Kanban boards for visual project management and note-taking capabilities.

### 4. Follow-Up Tracking
It monitors open threads and commitments, flagging when individuals have not responded and surfacing pending items that are about to be missed.

### 5. Daily Brief
Users receive a morning briefing summarizing the 3-5 most important items that require their attention—presenting decisions rather than chores. The brief includes drafted replies, pre-organized tasks, and calendar insights.

### 6. Always On
Unlike a human assistant, Alfred_ operates 24/7, including overnight, weekends, and holidays, eliminating the need for PTO or sick days, and processing information while you sleep.
