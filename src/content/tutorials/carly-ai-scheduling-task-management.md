## Step-by-Step Setup

### Step 1: Sign Up for Carly AI

1. Visit [usecarly.com](https://usecarly.com) and click "Get Started"
2. Enter your email address and phone number for verification
3. Check your email for a verification link and click it
4. Verify your phone number when prompted via SMS
5. Complete your profile with your name, timezone, and preferred working hours

### Step 2: Connect Your Calendars

Carly works best when it can see and manage your calendar:

1. In the Carly dashboard, go to "Integrations" → "Calendars"
2. Connect your Google Calendar and/or Outlook/Office 365 calendar
3. Grant Carly permission to read and write events
4. Set your default meeting duration and buffer times
5. Choose which calendars Carly should check for availability

### Step 3: Set Up Video Conferencing (Optional)

To enable Carly to add meeting links automatically:

1. Go to "Integrations" → "Video Conferencing"
2. Connect your preferred platform (Zoom, Microsoft Teams, or Google Meet)
3. Authorize Carly to create meetings on your behalf
4. Set default meeting settings (whether to require passwords, waiting rooms, etc.)

### Step 4: Configure Task Management

Carly can help you manage tasks and to-do lists:

1. Navigate to "Integrations" → "Task Management"
2. Connect your preferred task tool (Todoist, Microsoft To Do, etc.) or use Carly's built-in task manager
3. Set up how you want to receive task reminders (email, SMS, or both)
4. Configure your daily briefing timing (when you want to see your day's overview)

### Step 5: Set Up CRM Automation (For Sales-Focused Freelancers)

If you use a CRM like HubSpot or Salesforce:

1. Go to "Integrations" → "CRM"
2. Connect your CRM account (HubSpot, Salesforce, etc.)
3. Map which fields Carly should update when it captures new leads
4. Set up automation rules (e.g., "When I forward a lead email to Carly, create a new contact")
5. Test the integration by sending a sample lead to Carly

### Step 6: Customize Your Communication Preferences

Tell Carly how you prefer to communicate:

1. In "Settings" → "Communication", choose your primary channel (email or SMS)
2. Set up your signature and preferences for how Carly signs messages
3. Configure working hours so Carly knows when not to schedule meetings
4. Set up VIP contacts that Carly should prioritize or handle differently

## Using Carly AI in Your Daily Workflow

### Scheduling Meetings

The most powerful feature of Carly is its scheduling ability:

1. When someone emails you asking to meet, simply CC Carly (`carly@usecarly.com`) or forward the email to Carly
2. Carly will read the email, check your calendar, and suggest available times
3. Carly will respond to the proposer with options and handle all the back-and-forth
4. Once a time is agreed upon, Carly creates the calendar event and sends invites
5. If you connected video conferencing, Carly will automatically add meeting links

**Example workflow:**
- Client emails: "Can we meet next week to discuss the project?"
- You forward to Carly (or CC: carly@usecarly.com)
- Carly responds: "I checked your calendar. How about Tuesday at 2 PM or Thursday at 10 AM?"
- Client replies: "Tuesday at 2 PM works"
- Carly confirms, creates calendar event, adds video call link if configured, and notifies both parties

### Managing Tasks and To-Dos

Carly helps you stay on top of your tasks:

1. Email or text Carly: "Remind me to follow up with the Smith client tomorrow at 3 PM"
2. Carly creates a timed reminder and will notify you via your preferred channel
3. For recurring tasks: "Every Monday at 9 AM, send invoices to weekly clients"
4. Ask for your daily briefing: Text "brief" to Carly to get your day's overview
5. Mark tasks as complete: Reply "done" to a task reminder or tell Carly what you completed

### CRM Automation for Lead Management

If you're in sales or client services:

1. When you receive a lead inquiry, forward it to Carly
2. Carly extracts contact information and creates/updates the lead in your CRM
3. Carly can add notes from the conversation and set follow-up reminders
4. For email sequences: Tell Carly to "add this lead to my nurture sequence"
5. Carly can schedule follow-up calls or emails based on your CRM workflows

### Google Drive Integration

Carly can help you manage files without leaving your inbox:

1. Ask Carly: "Find the proposal draft for the Johnson project"
2. Carly searches your Google Drive and shares the file link
3. To share a file: "Share the latest logo concepts with the design team at their email"
4. Carly can create folders: "Make a new folder for Q2 marketing assets"
5. Carly manages permissions: "Give view access to the budget spreadsheet to my accountant"

## Advanced Features and Tips

### Creating Custom AI Agents

For repetitive specific tasks:

1. In Carly's dashboard, go to "AI Agents" → "Create Custom Agent"
2. Define the agent's purpose (e.g., "Recruiting Assistant" or "Invoice Follow-up Bot")
3. Set up triggers (specific email subjects, keywords in messages, etc.)
4. Define actions (send specific replies, update databases, schedule tasks, etc.)
5. Test with sample messages before activating

### Using Carly via SMS

When you're away from email:

1. Save Carly's phone number in your contacts (provided during setup)
2. Text your requests just like you would email
3. Carly understands natural language: "Move my 3 PM meeting to tomorrow morning"
4. You'll receive responses via SMS for quick interactions on the go

### Handling Group Scheduling

For meetings with multiple participants:

1. When scheduling a group meeting, include all participants in the email thread
2. CC Carly and ask: "Find a time that works for everyone next week"
3. Carly will check all attendees' calendars (if they're Carly users or have shared availability)
4. Carly proposes optimal times and handles all coordination
5. Once agreed, Carly sends calendar invites to all parties

### Setting Up Daily and Weekly Routines

Make Carly part of your regular workflow:

1. **Morning**: Text "brief" to Carly for your day's overview
2. **Throughout the day**: Forward meeting requests to Carly as they come in
3. **End of day**: Ask Carly: "What did I accomplish today?" for a quick review
4. **Friday afternoon**: Request a weekly summary and preview of next week
5. **Monthly**: Use Carly to review recurring tasks and automate anything repetitive

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Carly Doesn't Understand My Request

- Try rephrasing with more specific details
- Break complex requests into smaller steps
- Check if you're asking for something outside Carly's current capabilities
- Use Carly's help feature: Type "help" for examples of what Carly can do

### Calendar Conflicts or Double Bookings

- Ensure all your calendars are properly connected and syncing
- Check that Carly has permission to write to your primary calendar
- Review your availability settings in Carly's dashboard
- Consider setting up "focus time" blocks that Carly respects

### Integration Not Working

- Verify your connected account still has valid permissions
- Reconnect the integration if credentials have changed
- Check Carly's status page for any known service issues
- Ensure you're using the correct syntax for triggering integrations

### Missing SMS or Email Notifications

- Check your spam/junk folders for Carly messages
- Verify your phone number and email are correctly configured
- Ensure you haven't accidentally blocked Carly's number or email domain
- Check if you've set quiet hours that might be suppressing notifications
