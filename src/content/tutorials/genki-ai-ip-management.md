## Step 1: Sign Up and Onboard

1. Go to [genki.ai](https://genki.ai) and click **Get Started** or **Sign Up Free**.
2. Create an account using your email or Google account.
3. Complete the onboarding questionnaire about your creative focus (e.g., art, slogans, designs) and goals (e.g., passive income, brand building).
4. You’ll be taken to the Genki AI dashboard, where you can launch the “one-click full-chain workflow.”

## Step 2: Upload Your IP or Enter a Prompt

Genki AI accepts two inputs to start the IP-to-income process:

- **Image Upload**: Upload a high-quality image of your artwork, design, logo, or sketch.
- **Text Prompt**: Describe your idea in detail (e.g., “a vintage-style sunset over mountains with the text ‘Wanderlust’ in retro font”).

**Best Practices:**
- If uploading an image, ensure it’s clear and represents the core IP you want to monetize.
- If using a prompt, be specific about style, colors, text, and composition to guide the AI accurately.
- You can combine both: upload a base image and use a prompt to refine or add elements.

Click **Create New Project** and choose your input method.

## Step 3: Let Genki AI Generate IP Assets

Once your image or prompt is submitted, Genki AI’s autonomous engine begins:

1. **IP Asset Generation**: The AI analyzes your input and generates a suite of IP assets, including:
   - High-resolution versions of your design
   - Variations (color schemes, orientations, layouts)
   - Derivative assets (patterns, icons, badges) based on your core IP
2. **AI Refinement Loop**: Genki AI uses a closed learning loop to improve outputs based on what works best for similar IPs, ensuring market-ready designs.

You’ll see a preview of generated assets in the dashboard. You can:
- Approve assets as-is
- Request variations (e.g., “more vibrant colors” or “add a border”)
- Combine multiple assets into a new concept

**Tip:** Spend a few minutes reviewing the assets. Genki AI is autonomous, but your creative judgment ensures the output aligns with your vision.

## Step 4: Create Print-on-Demand (POD) Product Mock-ups

With approved IP assets, Genki AI automatically generates manufacturing-ready mock-ups for a range of POD products:

- Apparel: t-shirts, hoodies, tank tops, socks
- Accessories: mugs, phone cases, tote bags, hats
- Home & Wall: posters, canvas prints, pillows, blankets
- Digital: downloadable wallpapers, social media templates

**How it works:**
1. Select the product types you want to offer (you can start with a few and expand later).
2. Genki AI places your IP assets onto product templates, adjusting for print areas, seams, and color profiles.
3. You’ll see realistic mock-ups showing how the final product will look.

**Customization:**
- Adjust placement, size, and orientation of your design on each product.
- Choose product colors (if applicable) from available options.
- Add multiple designs to a single product (e.g., front and back of a t-shirt).

Once satisfied, confirm the product lineup. Genki AI now has manufacturing-ready files for each item.

## Step 5: Set Up Localized E-Commerce Pages

Genki AI doesn’t just create products—it builds your entire storefront, localized for global markets.

1. **Store Creation**: The AI generates a complete e-commerce store with:
   - Product pages for each item, including SEO-optimized titles, descriptions, and tags
   - Collections (e.g., “Summer Collection,” “Best Sellers”)
   - Essential pages: About, Contact, FAQ, Policy (shipping, returns)
2. **Localization**: For each target market you select (e.g., US, EU, Japan, Australia), Genki AI:
   - Translates product descriptions and metadata into the local language
   - Adapts SEO keywords for regional search trends
   - Sets up currency and tax settings automatically
   - Generates region-specific marketing copy

**You can:**
- Review and edit the AI-generated text to add your brand voice.
- Choose which markets to launch in (start with 1–3 and expand).
- Connect your own domain (requires a paid plan) or use a Genki.ai subdomain.

When you’re ready, activate the store. Genki AI provisions the storefront on secure, scalable hosting.

## Step 6: Generate Multi-Channel Marketing Materials

To drive traffic to your new store, Genki AI creates a suite of marketing assets:

- **Social Media Content**: AI-generated posts, captions, and hashtags for platforms like Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, and TikTok.
- **Promotional Videos**: Short talking-head or product showcase videos with AI avatars (optional voice cloning).
- **Email Templates**: Welcome sequences, product announcements, and abandoned cart emails.
- **Ad Creatives**: Pre-sized images and copy for Facebook Ads, Google Ads, and Pinterest.

**Process:**
1. Select the marketing channels you want to activate.
2. Genki AI uses your IP and product data to generate on-brand, ready-to-publish content.
3. Preview each asset and make tweaks if needed (e.g., adjust a caption, swap an image).

All marketing materials are designed to align with your store’s localization—so a Spanish Facebook ad will appear in Spanish, targeting users in Spain or Latin America.

## Step 7: Connect to Global Logistics & Fulfillment

The final step is activating the supply chain. Genki AI integrates with global print-on-demand and fulfillment networks:

1. **Production**: When an order is placed, Genki AI routes it to the nearest POD partner that carries your selected products.
2. **Printing & Shipping**: The partner prints the item on demand and ships it directly to the customer, anywhere in the world.
3. **Tracking & Updates**: Genki AI provides tracking numbers and delivery estimates, which you can automate to customers via email.
4. **Returns & Support**: Integrated return handling (where offered by POD partners) and basic customer inquiry routing.

**You don’t need to:**
- Manage inventory
- Handle printing equipment
- Negotiate with suppliers
- Deal with customs or international shipping

Genki AI handles it all, letting you focus on creation and promotion.
